Notes on coping mechanisms, therapy sessions, mindfulness practices, and self-care activities aimed at maintaining and improving mental well-being.

- Quantified Self:

  - Mood (measured on a scale from 1 to 10)
  - Anxiety levels
  - Stress levels
  - Depression symptoms
  - Happiness levels
  - Gratitude journaling
  - Meditation or mindfulness practice duration
  - Meditation sessions frequency
  - Moments of mindfulness throughout the day
  - Journaling (expressive writing about thoughts and emotions)
  - Positive affirmations practiced
  - Self-care activities (e.g., baths, massages, hobbies)
  - Time spent outdoors in nature
  - Social interactions (in-person or virtual)
  - Personal achievements or victories celebrated
  - Therapy or counselling sessions attended
  - Coping strategies employed during difficult times
  - Emotional triggers identified
  - Time spent worrying or ruminating
  - Mental health goals progress

- Mindfulness
  - Practice daily meditation.
  - Attend religious or spiritual gatherings regularly.
  - Spend time in nature.
  - Keep a gratitude journal.
  - Practice forgiveness.
  - Practice mindfulness in everyday activities.
  - Practice yoga or tai chi.
  - Reflect on your values and beliefs.
  - Attend a silent retreat.
