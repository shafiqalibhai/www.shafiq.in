# **Product Backlog: Professional Scrum Master (PSM) 1 Tutorial**  

## **Product Goal:**  
To create an engaging, comprehensive, and interactive online tutorial that helps aspiring Scrum Masters prepare for and pass the **Professional Scrum Master (PSM) 1** certification exam.  

## **Backlog Items (Ordered by Priority):**  

### **1. Core Scrum Framework (Must Have)**  
- **Description:** Explain Scrum theory, pillars, and values.  
- **Acceptance Criteria:**  
  - Covers Scrum Guide fundamentals.  
  - Includes interactive quizzes.  
  - Provides real-world examples.  

### **2. Scrum Roles & Responsibilities (Must Have)**  
- **Description:** Deep dive into Scrum Master, Product Owner, and Developers.  
- **Acceptance Criteria:**  
  - Clarifies accountabilities of each role.  
  - Compares traditional vs. Scrum roles.  
  - Includes role-play scenarios.  

### **3. Scrum Events (Must Have)**  
- **Description:** Breakdown of Sprint, Sprint Planning, Daily Scrum, Sprint Review, and Retrospective.  
- **Acceptance Criteria:**  
  - Explains purpose, participants, and outcomes.  
  - Provides best practices for facilitating events.  
  - Includes video simulations.  

### **4. Scrum Artifacts & Transparency (Must Have)**  
- **Description:** Covers Product Backlog, Sprint Backlog, and Increment.  
- **Acceptance Criteria:**  
  - Explains Definition of Done (DoD).  
  - Discusses backlog refinement techniques.  
  - Includes case studies on artifact misuse.  

### **5. PSM 1 Exam Preparation (Should Have)**  
- **Description:** Tips, mock exams, and common pitfalls.  
- **Acceptance Criteria:**  
  - 50+ practice questions with explanations.  
  - Time management strategies.  
  - Exam pattern breakdown.  

### **6. Advanced Scrum Mastery (Could Have)**  
- **Description:** Handling impediments, stakeholder management, and scaling Scrum.  
- **Acceptance Criteria:**  
  - Discusses conflict resolution techniques.  
  - Provides scaling frameworks (Nexus, LeSS).  
  - Includes expert interviews.  

### **7. Interactive Learning Tools (Could Have)**  
- **Description:** Flashcards, mind maps, and discussion forums.  
- **Acceptance Criteria:**  
  - Mobile-friendly flashcards.  
  - Community Q&A section.  
  - Downloadable cheat sheets.  

### **8. Certification & Next Steps (Should Have)**  
- **Description:** Post-certification guidance (PSM II, career paths).  
- **Acceptance Criteria:**  
  - Roadmap for advanced certifications.  
  - LinkedIn/Resume tips for Scrum Masters.  
  - Success stories from certified PSM holders.  

---  
**Refinement Notes:**  
- Prioritize based on PSM 1 exam weightage.  
- Include real-world case studies for practical understanding.  
- Gamification (badges, progress tracking) could enhance engagement.
