Navigating the world of enterprise architecture can be a daunting task, especially when you're not sure where to find reliable resources. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just dipping your toes into the architecture ocean, this curated list will help you stay ahead of the game.

### Agile Enthusiasts

**Agile Architect by Andrew Johnston**

- Questa Computing Ltd. runs this insightful blog, focusing on agile methodology within enterprise architecture.
- [Agile Architect Website](http://www.agilearchitect.org)

### Development and Trends

**Application Development Trends - Enterprise Architect Section**

- This site covers various trends and news in the enterprise architecture world.
- [ADT Magazine Enterprise Section](https://adtmag.com/articles/list/agile-architect.aspx)

### Governance & Strategy

**Architecture & Governance Magazine**

- This is your go-to magazine for governance topics related to enterprise architecture.
- [Architecture & Governance Magazine](http://www.architectureandgovernance.com/)

### Rule the Business World

**Business Rules Group**

- Focusing on the business aspects of architecture, this group aims to provide a comprehensive view of the enterprise architecture realm.
- [Business Rules Group](http://www.businessrulesgroup.org)

### Research & Studies

**Cutter Consortium**

- Specialized research centers offer various insights; note that most of the content is client-restricted.
- [Cutter Consortium](http://www.cutter.com/)

### Comprehensive Architecture

**Enterprise-Wide IT Architecture (EWITA)**

- A resource-rich platform, offering valuable perspectives and approaches.
- [EWITA](http://www.ewita.com)

### Industry Insights

**Forrester Research**

- Sections on Enterprise Architecture, CIO topics, and Business Process & Applications; content mostly limited to clients.
- [Forrester Research](http://go.forrester.com/)

### Academic Angle

**Institute For Enterprise Architecture Developments (IFEAD)**

- Founded by Jaap Schekkerman, this institute provides an academic approach to enterprise architecture.
- [IFEAD Website](https://www.nascio.org)

### Tools & Techniques

**Resources for Software Architects by Bredemeyer Consulting**

- Practical resources and advice for software architects.
- [Bredemeyer Consulting](http://www.bredemeyer.com)

### Best Practices

**Software Engineering Institute (SEI)**

- An institution that offers an array of guidelines, best practices, and training.
- [SEI](http://www.sei.cmu.edu)

### Standards & Frameworks

**The Open Group Architecture Forum**

- A forum that focuses on enterprise architecture standards and frameworks.
  
**Zachman Institute for Framework Advancement (ZIFA)**

- Promoting the Zachman Framework for enterprise architecture.
- [ZIFA](http://www.zifa.com)

Stay up-to-date with the ever-changing landscape of enterprise architecture by keeping these valuable resources at your fingertips. Happy learning!
